Project 404

Overview

Project 404 explores the dualism of mobility as it pertains to the physical and virtual realms. This dualism examines the attributes of function and form, drawing evidence that devices and active systems reflect most antiquated communication methods (e.g., cave paintings and hieroglyphs). The name, inspired by an error in response to a client request, questions the overall state of existence in pertinence to physical perception and mental projection in the context of mobility.

STUHL | The Edge of Tomorrow

Overview

Introducing the edge of the tomorrow, Stuhl. This contemporary and well crafted furniture needed a brand identity to complete such a compelling product. The name Stuhl fits best to represent a masterpiece manufactured by a german chair company. The structure of the chair and its design is sleek and sturdy. This chair is also light weight and made from sustainable materials.

LOLLAPALOOZA | Campaign Design

Overview

Lollapalooza is a music festival held annually in Chicago. It features main stream artist, bringing a range of music genres together. For this project I created a design campaign to encompass an EDM (electronic dance music) theme for the Lollapalooza brand. The campaign consist of a promotional poster, website, and logo design.
